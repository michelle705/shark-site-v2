The Problem

Most businesses aren't showing up in AI search at all

Even great businesses are being overlooked every day. AI only recommends businesses it can verify and trust—and most can't clear that bar.

96% of businesses are not recommended by AI

AI search surfaces only a handful of results per query. The vast majority of businesses never appear—regardless of quality.

Inconsistent information causes businesses to get skipped

When details don't match across platforms, AI treats the business as unverifiable—and moves on to the next option.

AI only recommends what it can verify and trust

Trust signals are the new ranking factor. Without them, even excellent businesses remain invisible at the moment of recommendation.

What Was Done

Using the AI Visibility Toolkit, the Chamber strengthened how it and its members show up across the internet

Clean, Consistent Business Information

Ensured accurate details across every platform AI relies on. Inconsistent information is one of the top reasons businesses get skipped—this closed that gap.

Stronger Digital Authority

Helped AI clearly understand and trust the Chamber as a local source. When the Chamber earns that trust, its members benefit directly from that visibility lift.

Aligned Member Visibility

Made it easier for AI to find, verify, and recommend member businesses—so the Chamber's credibility actively translates into member recommendations.
