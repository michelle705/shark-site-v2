Case Study: North Tampa Bay Chamber

96% of businesses are not being recommended by AI

In under 30 days, we shifted the Chamber from a traditional directory to a trusted primary source that AI search systems use to verify and recommend local businesses.

The Challenge

Traditional visibility is failing

AI systems (LLMs) only recommend businesses they can verify. If member data is inconsistent or outdated, those businesses get skipped entirely.

  • ✕ Inconsistent records across AI platforms
  • ✕ Lack of verified digital authority
  • ✕ Legacy search signals ignored by Generative AI
The Transformation

Strategic AI Readiness

We used the AI Visibility Toolkit to align the Chamber's data ecosystem, ensuring that AI models find, trust, and surface member information.

  • ✓ Cleaned business data across the AI ecosystem

  • ✓ Simplified AI verification for all members
